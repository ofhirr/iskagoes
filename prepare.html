Preparation

Amid the lowering of the Covid-19 quarantine restrictions in Laguna, residents of the City of Santa Rosa celebrated hard work and togetherness through the Sikhayan Festival. Where each Contestant is able to Prepare and rehearse in their respective Barangay.


Arlene B. Arcillas reiterated that the festival is not just a mere celebration but a way of showing everyone that amid the pandemic, Santa Rosa continues to deliver programs and services and it continues to thrive. Although it was held a month later than its usual date because of the restrictions, the annual celebration pushed through.


The city government also ensured that the health and safety guidelines of the Inter Agency Task Force for the Management of Emerging Infectious Diseases are strictly followed. All the participants and the viewing public present in the event were fully vaccinated. Social distancing and wearing of masks were observed as well, the mayor said.
